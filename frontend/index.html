HOW ANEXI WORKS

From order capture to automated decisioning

1) AI Call Agent

An intelligent voice agent calls new customers in seconds, confirms address and intent, detects hesitation signals, and logs structured call outcomes for every order.

2) Behavioral Brain

The risk engine scores each order using behavioral patterns, channel quality, phone consistency, and historical outcomes to estimate fulfillment reliability.

3) Trust Layer

A cross-order reputation system tracks buyer reliability over time, helping your team recognize trusted repeat customers and recurring risky profiles.

4) Smart Automation

Decision policies convert scores and call outcomes into actions: auto-confirm low-risk orders, auto-flag edge cases for review, and auto-reject high-risk orders before shipment.
